# UBUNTU CHAIN Brand Guidelines

## Visual Identity
Ubuntu Chain combines modern Web3 aesthetics with traditional African symbolism (Adinkra) to build trust and cultural resonance.

### Color Palette
- **Primary Orange:** `#FF6B00` (Energy, Innovation, Sunshine)
- **Deep Black:** `#1A1A1A` (Stability, Professionalism)
- **Soft White:** `#F8F9FA` (Clarity, Accessibility)
- **Success Green:** `#2ECC71` (Growth, Prosperity)

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Bold, geometric sans-serif (e.g., Montserrat or Arial Black).
- **Body:** Clean, legible sans-serif (e.g., Inter or Arial).

### Logo Usage
- **Icon:** Use in small spaces (favicons, profile pics).
- **Full Dark:** Use on dark backgrounds.
- **Full Light:** Use on light backgrounds.

### Tone of Voice
Empowering, community-focused, transparent, and technically robust. We use the phrase "I am because we are" (Ubuntu) as our guiding principle.
